Seismic Risk and Active Faults in Canada Discussion Group

A very short (30 minute) recurring discussion group for all things relating to active faults, paleoseismology, seismic risk, seismic hazard, and associated geohazards in Canada. All are welcome!

Schedule

  • Jan 26, 2022 Presentation by Tiegan Hobbs on the Canadian Seismic Risk Model (draft document available)
  • Feb 1, 2022 Discussion of D. Edgington's 2021 paper 'Planning for Earthquakes and Tsunamis: Lessons from Japan for British Columbia, Canada'



If you are interested in joining our discussion group please reach out via email.
